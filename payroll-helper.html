You

Set these once. Your name decides which shifts are claimed — enter it exactly as it appears in Brittco (e.g. “Franck Yeboue”). A shift counts whenever your name is in its Staff column, even if a coworker is listed too. To/From are used on every email.

Pay schedule

Pay is every 2 weeks and the app knows your pay days. It shows today, the current pay day, and the exact 2-week period — and on step 3 it tells you which weekly PDFs to pull. Use Prev / Next to move between pay periods.

Pay day auto-fills the period. Override the start or end only for an unusual period (e.g. one that includes the leading Sunday).

Client Rule Library

Mode:

Each client has a version-controlled rule profile with a confidence score and full history. Normal Payroll Mode uses only the approved active version and is read-only — rule changes are made in Rule Discovery Mode and require your explicit approval. Versions are never overwritten. A client with no approved rate is flagged; pay is never invented.

Night rate is fixed at 11pm–7am. Add each client and set their rate.

Upload Brittco attendance PDFs

Two formats work and are auto-detected. Recommended: the "Review Attendance" report — one file, filtered to your name and date range, listing each shift directly (sturdier). The older weekly grid export (3 Sun–Sat files) also works. The tool keeps only shifts dated inside the period.

Review extracted shifts

This is the safety step. Edit or exclude anything before generating the email. Times shown are rounded down to the whole hour (your rule). Cross-midnight shifts stay under their start date.

Back pay & adjustments

On payday, record what you were actually paid for a past period on the History tab. If you were underpaid, the shortfall shows up here so you can add it as back pay to this period. You can also add a manual adjustment (use a negative amount for a deduction). Adjustments are added into TOTAL PAY, each on its own line in the email.

Payroll audit report

Full audit trail for the current period: every shift's raw clock time → rounded whole hours → regular/overnight split → rate applied → dollars, plus which client rule version was used. Generate once the Review tab looks right.

Generate the audit report for the current period.

Backup & restore

Your data lives only in this browser. Back up saves everything — settings, clients & rates, saved periods, and the ledger — to one file on your device. Restore loads it back on this or any device. Do this after each payday so nothing is ever lost.

Past pay periods & reconciliation

Saved periods carry a full rule-version snapshot so reopening reproduces the exact numbers. On pay day, enter the amount actually paid to reconcile it against the calculated total — any shortfall flows to the Back-Pay Ledger below. Versions and history are never overwritten.
